# Runner Guide

Welcome to RaceLookup! This guide will help you navigate the platform and make the most of your running experience.

## Getting Started

### Create an Account

Start by creating a free account on RaceLookup to:
- Save your favorite events
- Track your race history
- Receive personalized recommendations
- Get exclusive offers and discounts

[Create Account](https://racelookup.com/register) | [Sign In](https://racelookup.com/login)

## Finding Events

### Search for Races

RaceLookup makes it easy to discover your next race. You can search for events using:

- **Date**: Filter events by specific dates or date ranges
- **Location**: Find events near you or in your dream destination
- **Keyword**: Search by event name, distance, or type

Use the search bar on the homepage to quickly find events that match your criteria.

### Browse Featured Events

Check out our curated selection of featured events on the homepage. These include:
- Popular races
- Upcoming events
- Special promotions

## Event Registration

### Register for a Race

1. Click on any event to view detailed information
2. Review event details including:
   - Date and location
   - Distance options (5K, 10K, Half Marathon, Marathon, etc.)
   - Registration fees
   - Event description
   - Course information
3. Click "Register" to begin the registration process
4. Complete the registration form with your information
5. Select any add-ons (t-shirts, medals, etc.)
6. Review and complete payment securely through Stripe

### Registration Features

- **Multiple Distance Options**: Many events offer multiple race distances
- **Team Registration**: Register with friends or join a team
- **Early Bird Pricing**: Take advantage of discounted early registration
- **Add-ons**: Customize your race experience with optional purchases

## Viewing Results

After race day, access your results:

1. Navigate to the event page
2. Click on "Results" or "Race Results"
3. Search for your name or bib number
4. View your official time and placement
5. Download or share your results

Results are typically available shortly after the race finishes and are updated in real-time.

## Tips for Runners

### Before the Race

- Register early to secure your spot and save money
- Review the course map and elevation profile
- Check the weather forecast
- Prepare your race day gear
- Review parking and transportation options

### On Race Day

- Arrive early to allow time for parking and bib pickup
- Warm up properly before the race
- Stay hydrated
- Follow race officials' instructions
- Enjoy the experience!

### After the Race

- Check your results on RaceLookup
- Download your race certificate (if available)
- View and purchase race photos
- Share your achievement on social media
- Plan your next race!
